IF Archive Mirrors

The canonical server for the IF-Archive is http://www.ifarchive.org/.

For many years, a group of volunteers hosted mirrors of the Archive. We are grateful to them! Some of these are still active, but we are no longer checking to see which ones are up to date.

  • USA: ifmirror.russotto.net (http)
  • USA: ifarchive.smallwhitehouse.org (http)
  • USA: ifarchive.info (http)
  • USA: ifarchive.ifreviews.org (http) (ftp)
  • USA: mirrors.ibiblio.org (http)
  • USA: gopher.661.org (gopher)
